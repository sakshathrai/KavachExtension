FAQS

Have questions? Here you will find answers most of the times, along with step-by-step guide.

What is browser extension?
A browser extension is a small software application that adds a capacity or functionality to a web browser.
How does the KAVACH extension detect dark patterns?
The extension uses algorithms to analyze the elements and behaviors of web pages on e-commerce platforms. It looks for patterns commonly associated with deceptive practices and alerts users when such patterns are detected.
How does the extension alert users to the presence of dark patterns?
When a dark pattern is detected on a webpage, the extension provides users with an alert or notification explaining the potentially deceptive tactic being used. This allows users to make more informed decisions while shopping online.
Does the extension support multiple languages, and how can I switch between them?
Yes, our extension supports multiple languages to cater to a diverse user base. You can easily switch between languages in the extension settings.
Will the extension detect dark patterns in different languages with the same accuracy?
The detection accuracy of dark patterns remains consistent across different languages supported by the extension=
What specific aspects of the extension can I customize, and how?
Users have the flexibility to customize various aspects of the extension, including detection sensitivity and specific types of dark patterns to focus on.
How does the extension visually represent data related to detected dark patterns?
Our extension presents data related to detected dark patterns using intuitive visualizations, making it easier for users to analyze and understand.
How does the extension verify the authenticity of prices, and what sources does it use?
The extension verifies the authenticity of prices by comparing them across different sources and monitoring for discrepancies or irregularities.
Does the ad detector feature differentiate between different types of advertisements?
Our ad detector feature distinguishes between different types of advertisements, including native ads and sponsored content, to help users identify potentially deceptive practices.
Does the extension offer the same level of functionality across different web browsers?
Our extension offers consistent functionality across different web browsers, ensuring a seamless experience for users regardless of their browser preference.
How does the redirection feature work, and can I specify custom redirection rules?
The redirection feature allows users to specify custom redirection rules when certain types of dark patterns are detected.
How can I provide feedback on the extension's performance or suggest improvements?
Users can provide feedback on the extension's performance and suggest improvements directly within the extension interface or through our website.
What measures are in place to protect user privacy while using the extension?
We prioritize user privacy and adhere to strict data protection measures to safeguard user information.
